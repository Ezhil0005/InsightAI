# Executive Command Center Specification

The **Executive Command Center** serves as the primary dashboard for corporate decision-makers.

## Layout & Components

1. **Executive Scorecard**: High-impact cards for Business Health, CSI, Churn, Revenue Risk, and Confidence levels.
2. **Operational Priorities**: Outlines top risk categories, customer counts, and category revenue risks.
3. **AI Recommendations**: Prominent display of action recommendations.
4. **Sub-category Checklist**: Checkboxes allowing managers to prioritize roadmaps.
