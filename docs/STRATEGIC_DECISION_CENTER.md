# Strategic Decision Center Specification

The **Strategic Decision Center** acts as an interactive playground where executives can model future investments, evaluate ROI, and compare different scenario matrices side-by-side.

## Scenarios Comparison Matrix

The platform compares four system states:

1. **Current State**: Baseline metrics calculated directly from ingested feedback.
2. **Best Case Scenario**: Assumes maximum operational efficiency upgrades are completed across all categories, recovering up to 80% of revenue at risk.
3. **Expected Scenario**: Models moderate complaint reduction (e.g. 35-40%) in the highest-priority risk category, resolving key pain points.
4. **Worst Case Scenario**: Assumes no action is taken, and current complaint volumes grow (modeling a 20% increase in churn and revenue exposure).

## Features & Visualizations
- **Outcome Grids**: Table displays of projected CSI, Churn, Revenue Risk, and Business Health across scenarios.
- **ROI Charts**: Interactive Plotly bar charts representing the recovered vs. remaining revenue at risk.
- **AI Recommendation**: Prescribes the single most cost-effective action based on ROI optimization.
